BIOGRAPHY
MY JOURNEY

In the intricate tapestry of artistic expression, there emerges a unique thread woven by the hands of Niyati Parekh an individual whose journey transcends the conventional boundaries of professions. Hailing from the vibrant landscapes of India, Niyati's story unfolds with an unconventional twist, as she dons the dual hats of an aeronautical engineer by education and a captivating mixed media artist by profession.

Born with a keen sense of curiosity, Niyati embarked on her academic journey in the realm of aeronautical engineering, navigating the intricacies of the skies and the principles that govern flight. However, the gravitational pull towards the world of art proved irresistible, leading her to explore the boundless realms of creativity that lie beyond the rigid confines of equations and calculations.

Venturing into the realm of mixed media art, Niyati Parekh discovered a profound connection between the technical precision ingrained in her engineering background and the unrestrained freedom found in artistic expression. Her works seamlessly fuse elements from diverse mediums, creating a visual symphony that mirrors the intricate dance of colors, textures, and emotions.

Niyati's artistic endeavors serve as a testament to the harmony she finds between the structured world of aeronautics and the limitless possibilities presented by the canvas. Each stroke of her brush narrates a tale of duality – a delicate balance between the analytical mind of an engineer and the boundless creativity of an artist.

As an ambassador of cultural diversity, Niyati Parekh's art reflects the rich tapestry of her Indian roots, incorporating traditional motifs and modern influences into a captivating visual narrative. Through her creations, she invites viewers to embark on a journey that transcends geographical borders, offering a glimpse into a world where engineering precision and artistic expression coexist in perfect harmony.

Join us as we delve into the captivating universe of Niyati Parekh – an aeronautical engineer who defies gravitational constraints to soar into the limitless skies of artistic imagination.
